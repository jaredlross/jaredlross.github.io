Welcome to the Hillandale Elementary PTA

The Parent-Teacher Association at Hillandale Elementary, located in Durham, North Carolina, serves as a powerful voice for all the children of Hillandale. We advocate for the education and well-being of every child, and we would love your help in making Hillandale the best place for your child to learn and grow!
